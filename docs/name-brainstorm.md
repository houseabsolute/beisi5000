# App name brainstorm

Current working name: **pramaper** ("practice makes perfect").
Other thrown out: **bassface5000**.

Pick whichever resonates; we can always iterate. Listed in rough buckets.

## Practice-discipline

- **Woodshed** — bassist/jazz slang for "going to practice a thing until it's solid". Universally understood by the audience; .com is taken but maybe `.app` or `getwoodshed`.
- **Practica** — short, evokes "practice"; minimal music association so it'd carry whatever brand you build on top.
- **Reps** — gym-borrowed but apt; very short.
- **Daily Pick** — descriptive; emphasizes the "roll something new" motion.

## Bass-themed punny

- **Lowend** — friendly, instantly identifies the niche, easy to spell.
- **bassface5000** — funny and memorable; the 5000 reads like an old-school synth/drum-machine model.
- **GroundFloor** — "everything starts on the bass"; double meaning of "ground floor of the band".
- **Foundation** — bass = harmonic foundation. A bit corporate but uncommon for music apps.
- **Bottom End** — too obvious, but stakes a clear claim.

## Music-theory themed

- **Roman Numeral** — a wink at chord/scale theory notation; quirky.
- **The Picker** — Random-picker as the central UX. Both bass-relevant (pickstyle vs fingerstyle) and literal.
- **One-Two-Three-Four** — the metronome count-in. Long but distinctive.

## Made-up / word-mashup

- **Etudo** — riff on "étude" (study piece); ends in -o which is friendly.
- **Scalebot** — practical, descriptive, but feels like a CS textbook.
- **Fretworks** — fret + works; suggests both the instrument and a workshop.
- **Strum & Drone** — actually wrong (no strumming for bass), skip.

## My favorites if I had to pick

If you want a name that signals the niche and "practice tool" simultaneously: **Woodshed**.
If you want playful and memorable: **bassface5000**.
If you want something that could outgrow "just bass" later: **The Picker** or **Etudo**.
